Thank you for using Knowledge Forum, here is how it works.

This site is for asking and answering questions, not for open-ended discussions. We encourage everyone to use "question" space for asking and “answer” for answering.

Despite that, each question and answer can be commented - the comments are good for the limited discussions.

Voting in Knowledge Forum helps to select best answers and thank most helpful users.

Please vote when you find helpful information, it really helps the Knowledge Forum community. Besides, you can @mention users anywhere in the text to point their attention, follow users and conversations and report inappropriate content by flagging it.
